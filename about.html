About

These internships allowed me to apply my knowledge in real-world settings, working alongside healthcare professionals to develop personalized nutrition plans and educate patients on healthy eating habits.

In addition to my background in nutrition, I am currently pursuing a Tecrix course in Generative AI. This course is helping me explore new technologies and their applications, broadening my skill set and knowledge base.
